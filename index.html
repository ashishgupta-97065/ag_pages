Will the rain spoil it?

June is wet season on the Andaman coast — rain most days, but as short heavy bursts (often afternoon/evening), not all-day grey. Mornings are frequently clear. And rain makes Krabi prettier — karsts go deep green, mist clings to the cliffs, and the 30 minutes after a downpour is the best light you'll get.

Do early

Boat trips & beaches in the morning, before afternoon storms build.

Hold flexible

Springs, temples, markets & the art museum are rain-proof — save for wet afternoons.

Skip in monsoon

Maya Bay / Phi Phi — the long open-water crossing turns rough.

Pack

Light rain jacket + dry bag for phones. Skip the umbrella — too windy.
