Will the rain spoil it?

June is wet season on the Andaman coast — expect rain most days, but as short heavy bursts (often afternoon/evening), not all-day grey. Mornings are frequently clear. And honestly: rain makes Krabi prettier — the karsts go deep green, mist clings to the cliffs, and the 30 minutes after a downpour is the best light you'll get.

Do early

Boat trips & beaches in the morning, before afternoon storms build.

Hold flexible

Hot springs, temples & markets are rain-proof — save them for wet afternoons.

Skip in monsoon

Phi Phi / Maya Bay — the 2-hr open-water crossing turns rough and miserable.

Pack

Light rain jacket + dry bag for phones. Skip the umbrella — too windy.
